UIT RGPV

Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), also known as University of Technology of Madhya Pradesh, is the State Technological University of Madhya Pradesh, situated in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India. It is a multi-campus affiliating university offering diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate, integrated and doctoral courses in fields like engineering, technology, pharmacy, management, architecture, design and applied sciences.
